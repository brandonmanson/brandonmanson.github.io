GitHub.io 2.0

Getting around to that redesign...

As part of our week 2 curriculum, we've been told to learn a thing or two about design and apply it to our GitHub pages! Here's what I've done thus far.

The Wireframe

It's a pretty basic, simple sketch I admit. However I had the design in my head already and wanted to get to coding instead of spending too much time sketching it out. As I go back and iterate, I will likely spend more time sketching.

Design Thoughts

My main goal for the design is to keep things looking simple and elegant by taking advantage of lots of negative space. Eventually I would like to add a two-column layout for my blog posts with featured images, but for this version I simply centered everything.

What's Next

There is tons of work yet to be done. Here is what's coming in the next iteration:

-Media Queries to make the site truly responsive

-Column layout for blog posts

-Social icons in the sticky header

-Updated blog template to reflect the new design of the home page, ensuring a consistent user experience throughout.

That's it for now. Be sure to check back soon for updates, and please give feedback in the group!
